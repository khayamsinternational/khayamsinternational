Privacy Policy

At Khayams International, we understand that as a manufacturer and exporter of custom apparel, our clients trust us with sensitive information, including designs, specifications, logos, and business data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect all confidential information shared with us during the course of business.

1. Confidentiality Commitment

We are fully committed to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of all materials and data provided by our clients. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Product designs and tech packs
  • Logos and branding elements
  • Material specifications
  • Business information and pricing models
  • Communication records
  • Sample approvals and modification notes
  • Client lists and market strategies (if shared)

2. Intellectual Property Protection

All designs, logos, and intellectual property (IP) shared with Khayams International remain the sole property of the client. We do not claim any ownership or usage rights over client-submitted materials.

We strictly do not share, replicate, or reproduce any custom design, artwork, or proprietary information for any third party, internal use, or public display — without explicit written permission from the client.

3. Non-Disclosure and Internal Control

All client-related data and designs are handled by authorized staff only.

Employees and subcontractors are bound by internal confidentiality obligations.

Data and samples are not discussed, disclosed, or shown to unrelated third parties.

Documents are securely stored and accessed only as required for production or communication.

If a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is requested, we are fully open to signing one at any stage of the business relationship.

4. Sample & Production Material Confidentiality

All physical samples, mock-ups, and prototypes created during development are:

  • Treated as confidential assets
  • Not displayed, photographed, or used for promotional purposes
  • Not reused or shared with other clients
  • Stored securely and discarded safely if not approved for production

If a sample is rejected or discontinued, we destroy it upon request or at the end of its retention period.

5. Data Usage and Storage

We only collect and store business-related information necessary to:

  • Communicate effectively
  • Fulfill custom orders
  • Arrange payments and shipments
  • Maintain professional records for repeat business

We do not use client data for advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or disclose your confidential information to any third parties, except in the following limited cases:

  • Shipping or logistics providers, solely for order delivery
  • Customs or trade authorities, if legally required for export compliance
  • Legal or tax advisors, if required for regulatory documentation

All such third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

7. Client Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a summary of any information we hold about your business or designs
  • Request the removal or destruction of unused samples, artwork, or data
  • Request restrictions on how your designs or branding are handled internally

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly using the details below.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our internal processes or legal obligations. Updated versions will be provided upon request or posted on our website.
