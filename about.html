About Khayams International

Decades of excellence in sportswear manufacturing and global distribution

Our Story

Founded in 2005 with just 5 skilled workers, Khayams International has grown from a small workshop in Sialkot to become one of Pakistan's leading sportswear manufacturers and exporters. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create high-quality athletic apparel that helps athletes perform at their best.

What started as a modest operation has blossomed into a thriving enterprise serving clients across multiple countries. Our first international partnership in 2011 marked a turning point, proving that our commitment to quality could compete on the global stage. Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, invested in cutting-edge technology, and built a team of over 300 dedicated professionals who share our passion for excellence.

Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 40,000 sqft manufacturing facility equipped with the latest machinery and technology. Our production capacity has grown exponentially, allowing us to serve clients worldwide while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that set us apart from larger competitors.

Why Choose Us

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We don't just manufacture sportswear - we craft performance-enhancing apparel that meets the exacting standards of professional athletes and sports enthusiasts worldwide. Our unique approach combines:

  • Decades of specialized experience in sportswear manufacturing
  • A vertically integrated production process for complete quality control
  • Dedicated research and development for continuous innovation
  • Ethical and sustainable manufacturing practices
